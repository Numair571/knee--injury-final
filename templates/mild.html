Knee Stretching Exercises

Exercise 1

Half Squats

  • Step 1: Keep your chest lifted and slowly lower your hips about 10 inches, as if you are sitting down into a chair.
  • Step 2: Plant your weight in your heels and hold the squat for 5 seconds.
  • Step 3: Push through your heels and bring your body back up to standing. Repeat.

Tip: Do not bend forward at your waist

Diet Recommendations:

Here are some general suggestions that might be considered for individuals with a mild knee fracture. These recommendations may vary depending on the severity of the injury, and professional advice should always be sought.

  • Range of Motion (ROM) Exercises: Gentle range of motion exercises can help maintain flexibility in the knee joint. Ankle pumps, straight leg raises, and seated knee extensions might be appropriate.
  • Isometric Exercises: These exercises involve contracting muscles without moving the joint. Isometric exercises for the quadriceps and hamstrings can help maintain muscle strength without putting too much stress on the knee.
  • Walking with Assistance: Initially, walking with crutches or a walker may be recommended. As you progress, your healthcare provider will guide you on when it's safe to increase weight-bearing.
