Diet Recommendations

Here are some general suggestions that might be considered for individuals with a healed knee fracture. These recommendations may vary depending on the severity of the injury, and professional advice should always be sought.

  • Antioxidants: Foods rich in antioxidants can help reduce inflammation and promote healing. Include plenty of fruits and vegetables such as berries, oranges, tomatoes, spinach, and bell peppers.
  • Whole Grains: Choose whole grains over refined grains for their higher nutrient content and fiber. Whole grains include foods like brown rice, quinoa, oats, and whole wheat bread.
